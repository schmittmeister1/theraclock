TheraClock Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

The short version: TheraClock collects no data. Nothing you enter ever leaves your device.

What the app does with your information

TheraClock is a calculator. The numbers you enter — start time, expected billed time, break time, and productivity percentage — are used only to calculate your expected clock-out time, entirely on your own device. They are never transmitted, uploaded, or shared with anyone, including us.

What is stored, and where

To save you retyping, the app remembers your most recent inputs in your browser's or device's local storage. This data stays on your device, is never sent anywhere, and is deleted if you clear the app's data or uninstall it.

What we do not do

TheraClock has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, no cookies, no tracking of any kind, and no third-party services. We do not collect personal information, health information, patient information, location data, or usage data — nothing.

Patient information

TheraClock is a scheduling aid for clinicians and does not ask for, process, or store any patient information. Please don't enter patient data into it; it has no fields intended for that purpose.

Children

TheraClock is a workplace tool intended for adult healthcare professionals. It is not directed at children and, since it collects nothing, collects nothing from anyone of any age.

Changes to this policy

If the app ever changes in a way that affects privacy, this page and the app's changelog will say so plainly. The current version of this policy always lives at schmittmeister1.github.io/theraclock/privacy.html.
